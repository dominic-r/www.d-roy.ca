SDKO Web Services (SWS)

SDKO Web Services (SWS) is my personal web services platform for my homelab and personal projects.

Infrastructure

My services are powered by servers located in Canadian data centers. Because I'm Canadian and live in Montreal, it wouldn't make sense to have infrastructure in US-West or other distant regions.

All servers are hosted on one of the following providers, whose terms and privacy policies also apply:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) - Canadian regions only
  • Linode - Canadian data centers
  • OVH - Canadian data centers

Several additional servers are part of the internal network infrastructure. For security reasons, their IPs and specifications are not publicly listed.

Logging and Data Retention

I maintain minimal logging practices:

  • Logs are stored and forwarded to a central logging server where they are analyzed and stored for a maximum of 30 days
  • No personal information is collected beyond what is necessary for service operation
  • All logs are stored exclusively on Canadian servers

Security Measures

I implement appropriate security measures for SWS, including encryption, regular updates, and industry standard practices. For security reasons, I don't publicly list all measures in detail.

For more detailed information about my privacy practices, please visit the Privacy Policy.
